Welcome to my blog! I’m excited to share with you one of my favorite bands, One OK Rock. If you’re into rock music with a mix of Japanese and English lyrics, this band might be the perfect fit for your playlist.



Why OOR?

One OK Rock has been a huge inspiration for me. Their unique sound, blending rock, punk, and post-hardcore, really speaks to me. Songs like "Renegades" and "Wherever You Are" have become anthems in my life, offering both energy and emotion that stick with me long after the music ends.

History

The history about how I met them? Hehe jk

One OK Rock was formed back in 2005 in Tokyo, and like many great bands, their journey started with a group of friends who just wanted to make music together. It all began when Toru Yamashita, the guitarist, had a dream of starting a rock band and invited his buddy Ryota Kohama, who actually wasn’t even a bassist back then—he was into hip-hop dancing! Pretty cool, right?

Later, Taka Moriuchi (my bf) joined as the lead vocalist, bringing an amazing vocal range and passion to the group. Eventually, drummer Tomoya Kanki completed the lineup, and the magic of One OK Rock started to take shape.

So why the name One OK Rock? It’s a fun story! They used to practice at one o’clock in the morning, and when said quickly in Japanese, “one o’clock” sounds like “One OK Rock” because of how the "L" and "R" sounds mix. They liked the sound of it and decided to stick with the name. It’s catchy and really represents the early days of the band when they were just finding their sound late at night.

One OK Rock hit the big leagues with their song "The Beginning" in 2012, which was featured in the live-action Rurouni Kenshin movie. That song not only blew up in Japan but also caught the attention of international audiences. Their style—a blend of rock, punk, and post-hardcore—combined with lyrics in both Japanese and English, makes their music universal, and that’s a big reason why so many people around the world love them today.

Their Best Songs

Based on several sources, including fan polls and critical reviews, here are some of the best songs by One OK Rock:

  1. "The Beginning" – One of their most famous tracks, featured in the *Rurouni Kenshin* movie. It’s often cited as a fan favorite for its high energy and emotional depth.
  2. "Mighty Long Fall" – A powerful song about the fear of losing someone, which gained massive popularity with its intense, rock-driven melody.
  3. "Wherever You Are" – This ballad has a more emotional, romantic side, making it a go-to song for fans who love heartfelt lyrics.
  4. "Clock Strikes" – Known for its soaring vocals and heavy guitar riffs, it's another hit that showcases the band’s rock roots.
  5. "We Are" – From their *Ambitions* album, this song became an anthem of self-confidence and empowerment, particularly for international fans.
  6. "Gravity" – This song holds a special place for me! It's not only beautifully composed but also conveys a deep emotional weight. The lyrics and the soaring melody (the guitar playing omg) just resonate on a personal level, making it one of my all-time favorites from One OK Rock.

Sources: AllMusic, Moshi Moshi Nippon
